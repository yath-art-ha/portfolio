endogenous industry Classification

Indian School of business Hyderabad

This was a natural language processing project in which I replicated the methodology in code given by Gerard Hoberg, Gordon Phillips in “Text-Based Network Industries & Endogenous Product Differentiation”. I tested and fine tuned my work on automation of 6000+ annual reports for 20 years after data extraction, data cleaning, and pre-processing for the scripted model. I used cosine similarity on the section given on Management Discussion and Analysis in firm's annual report as a measure to cluster firms into industries. Using these we can now implement a trading strategy to maximize returns.

predicting firm's political inclinations

Indian School of business Hyderabad

Under the guidance of Prof. Ritam Chaurey, Johns Hopkins SAIS and Prof. Shashwat Alok, Indian School of Business Hyderabad, I worked on inferring political connections of top 1000 firms through scraping, cleaning, and analysis of Political Action Committee data (USA) to infer the political color of firm (Democrats/Republicans) which was used to train a machine learning model which with the aid of sentiment analysis to get the tone of a media article on a firm and hence predicting its political color. Once we have a firms political colour we can implement a trading strategy during the time of eletions.

Impact of ayushman bharat (pm-jay)

Indian School of business Hyderabad

To measure the impact of Ayushman Bharat on the Economic Welfare of India in the implemented states, I worked on data visualization and collection of 30+ NFHS4 and NFHS5 variables of over 600 implemented and non-implemented states to compare its impact on districts lying on the border of implemented states with non implemented states, for report I collected per-capita health expenditure and represented it graphically for years 2015-2018 for clear analysis.
